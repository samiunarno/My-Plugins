Taxable Events

Buying, selling, or exchanging NFTs can trigger taxable events. Selling an NFT for a profit is subject to capital gains tax, while a loss could potentially be claimed as a capital loss.

Income Tax on NFT Sales

If you create and sell NFTs, the proceeds are treated as income and must be reported on your tax return, possibly as business income if done regularly or professionally.

Capital Gains Tax

When you sell an NFT, any increase in its value is subject to capital gains tax. The rate may depend on whether it's a short-term or long-term capital gain.

Royalties from NFT Resale

Some NFTs include royalties, allowing creators to earn a percentage every time the NFT is resold. These royalties are treated as income and should be reported for tax purposes.
